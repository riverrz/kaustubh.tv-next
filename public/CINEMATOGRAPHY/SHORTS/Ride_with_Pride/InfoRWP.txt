Synopsis: This short documentary follows Parampreet Rajput-Singh, founder of the Sikh Australian Motorcycle Club, in his quest to legalize riding motorcycles with turbans instead of helmets in order to adhere to religion. Parampreet must come face-to-face with his commitment to his faith while potentially risking responsibilities as a husband and a father.

A Film by Kylie Miller, Brynne Mckee, Hulu Nunokowa, Kaustubh 'Vick' Singh, Emily Tapanes
